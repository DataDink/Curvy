Why Curvy?

I know, I know. Enough custom frameworks already. I created Curvy for the fun of creating. There is nothing special to see here other than my own spin on things.

Curvy's creation was a side note in a project meant to demonstrate my javascript abilities. I took what I liked about Angular and reshaped it to fit into what I felt was a more natural pattern for web front-end technologies.

I do plan on using Curvy, at most, in some of my side projects here and there in order to help further improve it. I continue to develop Curvy is because, like having a plant, I like to watch it grow.
